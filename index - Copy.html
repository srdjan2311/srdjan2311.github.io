Our Mission:

Turn your data into a business advantage.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning based solutions are slowly becoming industry standards in modern products and services in every niche. Machine learning algorithms use a variety of computational methods to "learn" information directly from data without relying on a predefined equation as a model and adaptively improve their performance as the number of available learning samples increases.
We aim to empower your business by utilizing the full potential of your data. We do this thoughtfully by applying not only the latest methods of artificial intelligence and machine learning, but also classical statistics techniques and exploratory data analysis.

Research

Research at Bloomsbury AI actively participates in the company vision by developing and extending machine learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) methods. We have a focus on impactful research that can be moved into production as quickly as possible.

Sample Research Areas

Machine reading methods that understand written text to answer questions about its explicit content, across a broad range of domains.

Robust question answering methods that understand when different questions ask for the same answer.

Reasoning methods that can infer answers from text that are not explicitly stated.

Dialog methods that acquire expert knowledge and deliver it to customers through conversations.

Methods that automatically explain why the agent made its choices.
